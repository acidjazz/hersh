Dangerous Property Conditions

Property can be dangerous for a number of reasons, including the failure by an owner to warn of a dangerous condition, rules and regulations that are unsafe, a defective product or item, like a ladder, on the property, failure by the owner to have security on site, or dangerous conditions on a roadway, to name a few.

We have successfully represented people who have sustained injuries because a property or environment they entered was unsafe, including worksites, construction sites or roadways. We also consider that an “on the job injury” may have been caused by someone or some business entity, not just your employer.

We have worked on cases involving a man who fell from a defective ladder attached to a building, a brain injury from drug overdose in a university residence because of policies and procedures in the residence, death from assault in a nightclub without sufficient security, and injuries from a defective guardrail on a roadway piercing a vehicle.
